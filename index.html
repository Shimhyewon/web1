Overview of the project

The exhibition hall is a square. They have the same interest and purpose in the same space at the same time.

At the same time, it is also a small group of people with different ages, different backgrounds, and different academic knowledge. By presenting a new paradigm, taking into account the spatial characteristics of these museums, the company intends to design an exhibition platform that can broaden the spectrum of experiences. This project is a location-based MR interaction platform development project to provide a vivid experience to museum visitors. It is a research project for developing museum contents suitable for modern digital content consumption taking into account the characteristics of oral communication. The concept of active participation exhibition is to introduce various story compositions and live museum experience exhibition technology that can be interpreted in a new way for experiences.
